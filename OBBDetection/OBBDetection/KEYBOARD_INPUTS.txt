These are they key inputs after startup:

Q, ESCAPE - QUIT

C - TURN RENDERING OF THE CONVEX HULL AT THE CURRENT OBB DEPTH ON / OFF

O - TURN RENDERING OF THE OBB DIMENSIONS AT THE CURRENT OBB DEPTH ON / OFF

M - TURN RENDERING OF THE MODELS ON / OFF

P - PAUSE THE PHYSICS INTEGRATOR

L,<,> - TRAVERSE THE OBBTREE WHEN RENDERING IT (L = PARENT, < = LEFT CHILD, > = RIGHT CHILD)

SHIFT - TOGGLE RUNNING

WSAD - MOVE THE CAMERA

F1 - Full screen
